Lasagna Recipe

This recipe will be using lots of parmesan cheese and a ton of ricotta cheese to drive creaminess factor to the maximum. We want to use fresh home made pasta and using canned San Marzano tomatoes hand squished for an authentic taste.

Ingredients

  • San Marzano tomatoes
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Make the pasta
  2. Pour our tomatoes and squish by hand
  3. Cook and drain the pasta to right before al dente
  4. Dry the pasta and start layering in this order:
    1. Pasta
    2. Ricotta cheese
    3. parmesan cheese
  5. Cook the pasta at 350°F for 25 minutes
