BLT Sandwich

This BLT sandwich will be very easy to fast for on the go fuel.

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • White bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps:

  1. Cook the bacon until cooked through entirely and to desired doneness.
  2. Use a small bit of bacon grease to toast bread with.
  3. Layer your bacon, lettuce, and tomato into whichever order desired.
