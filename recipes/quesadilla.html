Quesadilla Recipe:

This wil be a very simple and easy to cook recipe that will use pre-made tortillas and sliced cheese

Ingredients:

  • American cheese
  • Cooked tortillas
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Heat pan on medium-low
  2. Place tortilla on pan
  3. Place slices of american cheese on the tortilla while in the pan
  4. Fold the tortilla in half
  5. flip after the tortilla turns a golden brown and cook the other side.
